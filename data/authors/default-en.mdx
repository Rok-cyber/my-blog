I study and build **AI systems and governance**: the structures that allow increasingly capable agents to operate safely, accountably, and usefully in the real world.

Software is the implementation layer. Cities, institutions, and public life are where many of these ideas can be tested against reality.

## What I explore

- **AI markets and technology shifts** — the signals behind adoption, investment, competition, and new capabilities
- **AI systems and governance** — authority, accountability, auditability, safety, and measurable value
- **Future scenarios** — how agents may reshape work, institutions, policy, and everyday life

The English edition is published every two weeks. Each outlook separates observable signals from interpretation and longer-range forecasts, so uncertainty stays visible rather than being disguised as certainty.
